# Invoice Templates and Standards

## Standard Invoice Template

### Basic Structure
```
[COMPANY LOGO]

[COMPANY NAME]
[ADDRESS LINE 1]
[ADDRESS LINE 2]
[PHONE] | [EMAIL] | [WEBSITE]

INVOICE #: [INVOICE NUMBER]
DATE: [INVOICE DATE]
DUE DATE: [DUE DATE]

BILL TO:
[CLIENT NAME]
[CLIENT ADDRESS]
[CLIENT ADDRESS CONTINUED]

ITEMS:
[DESCRIPTION]          [QTY]  [RATE]     [AMOUNT]
--------------------------------------------------
[Item Description]     [Qty]  [$Rate]    [$Amount]
[Item Description]     [Qty]  [$Rate]    [$Amount]

SUBTOTAL:             $[Amount]
TAX ([Tax Rate]%):    $[Amount]
DISCOUNT:             -$[Amount]
TOTAL:                $[Total Amount]

PAYMENT TERMS: [Payment Terms]
THANK YOU FOR YOUR BUSINESS!
```

## Tax Categories

### Standard Tax Rates
- Federal Tax: As per jurisdiction
- State Tax: As per state regulations
- Sales Tax: On applicable items
- VAT: For international clients (if applicable)

### Tax-Exempt Categories
- Certain services (varies by jurisdiction)
- Some software licenses
- Educational materials

## Payment Terms

### Standard Terms
- Net 30: Payment due within 30 days
- Net 15: Payment due within 15 days
- Due on Receipt: Payment immediately upon receipt
- Net 60: Payment due within 60 days (for large clients)

### Early Payment Discounts
- 2% discount if paid within 10 days (Net 30)
- 1% discount if paid within 7 days (Net 15)

## Late Payment Policies

### Grace Periods
- 0-7 days: No penalty
- 8-15 days: Friendly reminder
- 16-30 days: 1.5% monthly service charge
- 31+ days: 2% monthly service charge + possible legal action

### Collections Process
1. Automated reminder at 15 days past due
2. Personal follow-up at 30 days past due
3. Collection agency referral at 90 days past due

## Approval Thresholds

### Automatic Approval
- Invoices under $500: Auto-approve
- Repeat clients with good payment history: Auto-approve up to $2,500
- Standard service invoices: Auto-approve up to $1,000

### Requires Manager Approval
- Invoices between $500-$5,000
- First-time client invoices over $1,000
- Custom project invoices over $2,000

### Requires Executive Approval
- Invoices over $5,000
- International client invoices over $2,500
- Any invoice with special payment terms

## Invoice Validation Rules

### Required Fields
- Client name and complete address
- Valid email for electronic delivery
- Correct tax calculations
- Proper invoice numbering sequence
- Clear description of goods/services

### Prohibited Practices
- Backdating invoices
- Double-billing for same service
- Charging for unapproved work
- Incorrect tax application
- Misrepresenting services provided

## Currency and International Standards

### Domestic Invoicing
- USD for US-based clients
- Proper currency symbol placement
- Two decimal places for cents

### International Invoicing
- Local currency conversion at invoice date
- Exchange rate disclosure
- VAT/GST inclusion as required
- Multi-currency support for recurring clients

## Electronic Invoice Standards

### Required Format
- PDF with embedded fonts
- Print resolution (300 DPI)
- OCR-enabled for text extraction
- Accessible to screen readers

### Security Features
- Digital watermark
- Sequential numbering system
- Encrypted transmission
- Audit trail preservation

## Delivery Methods

### Electronic Delivery
- Email with PDF attachment
- Client portal access
- Accounting software integration
- Automated delivery confirmation

### Physical Delivery
- Certified mail for high-value invoices
- Standard mail for routine invoices
- Express shipping when requested
- Signature confirmation for >$10,000 invoices

## Client Communication Standards

### Pre-Invoice Communication
- Work completion confirmation
- Scope verification
- Time/materials approval (if applicable)

### Post-Invoice Communication
- Delivery confirmation
- Payment expectation setting
- Follow-up schedule establishment

## Compliance Requirements

### Legal Compliance
- Jurisdiction-appropriate tax calculations
- Required legal disclaimers
- Proper business licensing information
- Accurate tax ID/EIN display

### Industry Compliance
- Professional service standards
- Data privacy regulations (GDPR, CCPA)
- Industry-specific billing requirements
- International trade compliance (for global clients)
