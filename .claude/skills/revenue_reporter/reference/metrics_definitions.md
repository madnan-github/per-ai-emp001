# Revenue Metrics Definitions

## Core Revenue Metrics

### Total Revenue
- **Definition**: Sum of all revenue earned during the reporting period
- **Formula**: Σ(All revenue transactions)
- **Frequency**: Daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, annually
- **Components**: Product sales, service revenue, subscription revenue, other income

### Recurring Revenue
- **Definition**: Predictable revenue that occurs regularly (monthly, quarterly, annually)
- **Formula**: Σ(Subscription revenue + Contract renewals + Retainer fees)
- **Frequency**: Monthly, quarterly, annually
- **Components**: Monthly recurring revenue (MRR), Annual recurring revenue (ARR)

### Average Revenue Per User (ARPU)
- **Definition**: Average revenue generated per customer
- **Formula**: Total Revenue ÷ Number of Active Customers
- **Frequency**: Monthly, quarterly
- **Components**: Customer count, revenue by customer segment

### Revenue Growth Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage change in revenue over time
- **Formula**: ((Current Period Revenue - Previous Period Revenue) ÷ Previous Period Revenue) × 100
- **Frequency**: Monthly, quarterly, annually
- **Components**: Month-over-month, quarter-over-quarter, year-over-year

## Customer Metrics

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- **Definition**: Average cost to acquire a new customer
- **Formula**: Sales & Marketing Expenses ÷ Number of New Customers Acquired
- **Frequency**: Monthly, quarterly
- **Components**: Marketing spend, sales team costs, advertising costs

### Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
- **Definition**: Predicted net profit attributed to the entire future relationship with a customer
- **Formula**: (Average Purchase Value × Purchase Frequency × Customer Lifespan)
- **Frequency**: Quarterly, annually
- **Components**: Average order value, purchase frequency, churn rate

### Churn Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of customers who stop using the product/service during a given time period
- **Formula**: (Customers Lost During Period ÷ Total Customers at Start of Period) × 100
- **Frequency**: Monthly, quarterly
- **Components**: Voluntary churn, involuntary churn, revenue churn

## Profitability Metrics

### Gross Revenue
- **Definition**: Total revenue before deductions
- **Formula**: Sum of all sales and service revenue
- **Frequency**: Daily, weekly, monthly
- **Components**: Product revenue, service revenue, other revenue

### Net Revenue
- **Definition**: Revenue after returns, refunds, and discounts
- **Formula**: Gross Revenue - Returns - Refunds - Discounts
- **Frequency**: Monthly, quarterly, annually
- **Components**: Gross revenue, refunds, chargebacks, promotional discounts

### Gross Margin
- **Definition**: Revenue minus cost of goods sold (COGS)
- **Formula**: (Revenue - COGS) ÷ Revenue × 100
- **Frequency**: Monthly, quarterly, annually
- **Components**: Revenue, direct costs, production costs

### Operating Margin
- **Definition**: Revenue minus operating expenses
- **Formula**: (Revenue - Operating Expenses) ÷ Revenue × 100
- **Frequency**: Monthly, quarterly, annually
- **Components**: Revenue, operating expenses, overhead costs

## Sales Metrics

### Sales Velocity
- **Definition**: Speed at which deals move through the sales pipeline
- **Formula**: (Number of Closed Deals ÷ Time Period) × Average Deal Size
- **Frequency**: Monthly, quarterly
- **Components**: Pipeline velocity, deal size, conversion rates

### Lead-to-Customer Conversion Rate
- **Definition**: Percentage of leads that convert to paying customers
- **Formula**: (Number of Converted Leads ÷ Total Leads) × 100
- **Frequency**: Monthly, quarterly
- **Components**: Marketing qualified leads, sales qualified leads, conversions

### Sales Cycle Length
- **Definition**: Average time from lead generation to closed deal
- **Formula**: Average time between lead creation and deal closure
- **Frequency**: Monthly, quarterly
- **Components**: Lead age, deal progression, closing time

## Financial Ratios

### Revenue Concentration
- **Definition**: Percentage of revenue from top customers
- **Formula**: (Revenue from Top N Customers ÷ Total Revenue) × 100
- **Frequency**: Quarterly, annually
- **Components**: Top customer revenue, total revenue, concentration risk

### Revenue Efficiency
- **Definition**: Revenue generated per dollar spent on customer acquisition
- **Formula**: (Annual Recurring Revenue ÷ Customer Acquisition Cost)
- **Frequency**: Quarterly
- **Components**: ARR, CAC, payback period

### Revenue Predictability
- **Definition**: Percentage of revenue that is predictable and recurring
- **Formula**: (Recurring Revenue ÷ Total Revenue) × 100
- **Frequency**: Monthly, quarterly
- **Components**: Recurring revenue, one-time revenue, contract terms

## Industry-Specific Metrics

### Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
- **Definition**: Predictable revenue expected monthly from subscription customers
- **Formula**: Σ(Customer Monthly Subscription Values)
- **Frequency**: Monthly
- **Components**: New MRR, Expansion MRR, Contraction MRR, Churned MRR

### Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)
- **Definition**: Predictable annual revenue from subscription customers
- **Formula**: MRR × 12
- **Frequency**: Annually
- **Components**: MRR, multi-year contracts, annual billing

### Bookings
- **Definition**: Value of signed contracts during a period (recognized over time)
- **Formula**: Σ(Value of contracts signed in period)
- **Frequency**: Quarterly, annually
- **Components**: New bookings, expansion bookings, contraction bookings

## Reporting Standards

### GAAP Compliance
- **Revenue Recognition**: Follow ASC 606 guidelines
- **Timing**: Recognize revenue when control transfers to customer
- **Allocation**: Allocate transaction price to performance obligations
- **Disclosure**: Provide required revenue disclosures

### Internal Reporting
- **Consistency**: Use consistent definitions across reports
- **Accuracy**: Ensure data accuracy and completeness
- **Timeliness**: Generate reports within defined timeframes
- **Accessibility**: Ensure appropriate stakeholders have access

## Benchmark Comparisons

### Industry Benchmarks
- **SaaS**: MRR growth rate, CAC payback period, net revenue retention
- **E-commerce**: Customer acquisition cost, lifetime value ratio, gross margin
- **Services**: Utilization rates, billable hours, revenue per employee
- **Manufacturing**: Capacity utilization, inventory turnover, gross margins

### Internal Benchmarks
- **Historical Performance**: Compare to previous periods
- **Budget Targets**: Compare to forecasted values
- **Goal Achievement**: Measure against strategic objectives
- **Seasonal Adjustments**: Account for seasonal variations
