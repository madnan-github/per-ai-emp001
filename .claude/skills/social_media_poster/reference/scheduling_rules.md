# Social Media Scheduling Rules

## Optimal Posting Times

### LinkedIn
- Tuesday-Thursday: 8-10 AM, 12-2 PM, 7-9 PM
- Avoid weekends and holidays
- Target business professionals in EST/CST time zones

### Twitter
- Tuesday-Thursday: 8 AM, 7-9 PM
- Friday: 5-6 PM
- Avoid Sunday mornings

### Facebook
- Tuesday-Thursday: 1-3 PM
- Wednesday: 3 PM
- Avoid weekends (lower engagement)

### Instagram
- Tuesday-Thursday: 11 AM, 7 PM
- Friday: 10-11 AM, 2-3 PM
- Saturday: 11 AM, 7-9 PM

## Content Distribution Strategy

### Daily Distribution
- LinkedIn: 1 post per day (preferably morning)
- Twitter: 1-3 tweets per day
- Facebook: 1 post every 1-2 days
- Instagram: 1 post every 1-2 days, multiple stories daily

### Weekly Distribution
- Monday: Motivational/weekly preview posts
- Tuesday: Educational content
- Wednesday: Mid-week engagement posts
- Thursday: Industry insights
- Friday: Weekend preparation/light content
- Saturday: Casual/lifestyle content
- Sunday: Light content, weekend wrap-up

## Hashtag Strategy

### Platform-Specific Limits
- LinkedIn: 3-5 hashtags max
- Twitter: 1-2 hashtags max
- Facebook: 1-5 hashtags (not heavily used)
- Instagram: 10-15 hashtags (mix of popular and niche)

### Hashtag Categories
- Branded hashtags (#CompanyName)
- Industry hashtags (#IndustryTerm)
- Campaign hashtags (#CampaignName)
- Community hashtags (#LocalBusiness)
- Trending hashtags (when relevant)

## Approval Thresholds

### Automatic Approval
- Scheduled promotional posts (under $500 value)
- Educational content posts
- Employee highlight posts
- Curated industry news shares

### Requires Approval
- Campaign launches (budget > $500)
- Controversial topics or positions
- Crisis communication
- Celebrity/brand partnership announcements
- Financial results disclosure

### Escalation Required
- Legal-related content
- Competitive comparisons
- Political statements
- Sensitive industry issues
- Executive communication

## Content Quality Standards

### Mandatory Checks
- Grammar and spelling verification
- Image quality and resolution
- Link functionality
- Brand consistency
- Message clarity

### Prohibited Content
- Offensive language
- Copyright-infringing material
- Misleading claims
- Discriminatory content
- Conflicting brand messages

## Performance Monitoring

### Metrics to Track
- Engagement rate (likes, comments, shares)
- Reach and impressions
- Click-through rate
- Conversion attribution
- Follower growth rate

### Performance Thresholds
- Engagement rate < 1%: Content needs adjustment
- Negative sentiment > 10%: Immediate review required
- Reach decline > 20%: Investigate and optimize
- Click-through rate < 0.5%: Content reconsideration
