## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### LinkedIn Best Practices
- Posts between 1,500-2,000 characters perform well
- Use 1-3 relevant hashtags
- Engage with comments within the first hour
- Share industry insights and company achievements

### Twitter Best Practices
- Keep tweets under 280 characters
- Use 1-2 hashtags maximum
- Include visuals when possible
- Retweet relevant industry content

### Facebook Best Practices
- Mix of promotional and educational content (70/30 ratio)
- Post at optimal times (weekday mornings/evenings)
- Encourage user-generated content
- Use Facebook Groups strategically

### Instagram Best Practices
- Use Stories for behind-the-scenes content
- Leverage Reels for increased visibility
- Maintain consistent visual brand identity
- Use 5-10 relevant hashtags
