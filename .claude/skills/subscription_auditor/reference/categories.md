# Subscription Categories and Classifications

## Service Categories

### Business Software
- **Productivity Suites**: Office 365, Google Workspace, Dropbox, Box
- **Communication Tools**: Slack, Teams, Zoom, RingCentral
- **Project Management**: Asana, Trello, Monday.com, Notion
- **Design Tools**: Adobe Creative Suite, Figma, Canva, Sketch

### Infrastructure & Development
- **Cloud Services**: AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, DigitalOcean
- **Development Tools**: GitHub, GitLab, Jira, Confluence
- **Monitoring Services**: Datadog, New Relic, Sentry, Pingdom
- **Security Services**: Okta, Duo, LastPass, 1Password

### Marketing & Sales
- **Email Marketing**: Mailchimp, Constant Contact, Klaviyo
- **CRM Systems**: Salesforce, HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho CRM
- **Analytics Tools**: Google Analytics, Mixpanel, Amplitude
- **Social Media**: Hootsuite, Buffer, Sprout Social, Later

### Finance & Accounting
- **Accounting Software**: QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks, Sage
- **Payment Processing**: Stripe, PayPal, Square, Shopify Payments
- **Expense Management**: Expensify, Concur, TripActions, Ramp
- **Payroll Services**: Gusto, ADP, Paychex, Rippling

### HR & Operations
- **HRIS Systems**: BambooHR, Workday, Greenhouse, Lever
- **Recruiting Tools**: Indeed, LinkedIn Recruiter, ZipRecruiter
- **Time Tracking**: Toggl, Harvest, Clockify, Deputy
- **Background Checks**: Checkr, GoodHire, Sterling, HireRight

## Cost Tiers

### Tier 1: Critical Services ($500+/month)
- **Enterprise Applications**: Core business systems
- **Infrastructure Services**: Mission-critical hosting/operations
- **Compliance Tools**: Legal/regulatory requirements
- **Security Systems**: Enterprise security solutions

### Tier 2: Important Services ($100-$499/month)
- **Productivity Tools**: Team collaboration software
- **Marketing Platforms**: Customer acquisition tools
- **Development Infrastructure**: Hosting and deployment
- **Business Intelligence**: Analytics and reporting tools

### Tier 3: Standard Services ($25-$99/month)
- **Specialized Tools**: Department-specific applications
- **Support Services**: Customer service and support tools
- **Utility Software**: Administrative and utility tools
- **Trial Services**: Temporary or trial subscriptions

### Tier 4: Low-Cost Services (<$25/month)
- **Freemium Upgrades**: Basic upgrades to free tools
- **Individual Tools**: Personal productivity applications
- **Experimental Services**: Trial or pilot programs
- **Ancillary Services**: Supplementary tools and utilities

## Usage Classification

### High-Utilization Services (>75% usage)
- **Core Business Functions**: Daily-used critical systems
- **Team-Wide Adoption**: Services used by majority of staff
- **Integration Points**: Services integrated into workflows
- **Regular Feature Use**: All/most features actively used

### Medium-Utilization Services (25%-74% usage)
- **Department-Specific**: Used by specific teams or departments
- **Project-Based**: Used for specific projects or campaigns
- **Feature-Limited**: Some features used, others idle
- **Seasonal Usage**: Varies based on business cycles

### Low-Utilization Services (<25% usage)
- **Underused Premium**: Paid features rarely accessed
- **Infrequently Used**: Occasional access for specific tasks
- **Forgotten Subscriptions**: Services no longer needed
- **Over-Provisioned**: Higher tier than necessary

## Vendor Classification

### Primary Vendors (Single Source)
- **Microsoft**: Office suite, Azure, Teams
- **Google**: Workspace, Analytics, Cloud
- **Adobe**: Creative Suite, Marketing Cloud
- **Salesforce**: CRM, Marketing Cloud, Service Cloud

### Alternative Vendors (Competing Solutions)
- **Slack vs. Teams**: Communication platform competition
- **Asana vs. Monday.com**: Project management options
- **Mailchimp vs. Klaviyo**: Email marketing alternatives
- **QuickBooks vs. Xero**: Accounting software options

### Specialized Vendors (Niche Solutions)
- **Industry-Specific**: Tailored to specific business needs
- **Integration Partners**: Deep integration with other tools
- **Compliance Focused**: Regulatory or security requirements
- **Emerging Solutions**: New market entrants with innovative features
