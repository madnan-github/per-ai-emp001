# Subscription Optimization Rules

## Identification Criteria

### Unused Service Detection
- **Zero Usage Threshold**: Services with no activity for 90+ days flagged for review
- **Low Usage Pattern**: Services with <10% utilization over 30-day period
- **Duplicate Services**: Multiple services with overlapping functionality
- **Underutilized Premium Features**: Paid premium features rarely used

### Cost-Benefit Analysis
- **Cost vs. Usage Ratio**: Services costing >$50/month with <25% usage
- **ROI Calculation**: Annual cost compared to business value delivered
- **Alternative Solutions**: Cheaper alternatives with equivalent functionality
- **Team Adoption Rate**: Services adopted by <25% of eligible team members

## Categorization System

### Priority Levels
- **Critical**: Services essential for business operations (highest priority)
- **Important**: Services with significant business value (monitor closely)
- **Marginal**: Services with questionable value (review for elimination)
- **Unused**: Services with zero or minimal usage (flag for cancellation)

### Review Categories
- **Immediate Action Required**: Cancel unused services within 7 days
- **Manager Review**: Services requiring management approval to cancel
- **Alternative Investigation**: Find cheaper alternatives to expensive services
- **Usage Training**: Team training needed to maximize service value

## Optimization Recommendations

### Immediate Actions
- **Cancel Unused Subscriptions**: Terminate services with no usage for 90+ days
- **Downgrade Over-Provisioned Services**: Reduce plan tiers for underutilized services
- **Consolidate Duplicate Services**: Merge redundant services into single solution
- **Negotiate Better Rates**: Leverage volume discounts for enterprise agreements

### Long-term Strategies
- **Quarterly Reviews**: Systematic review of all subscriptions every quarter
- **Usage Tracking**: Implement tools to monitor service utilization
- **Budget Allocation**: Assign department budgets for subscription expenses
- **Approval Process**: Require approval for subscriptions over $50/month

## Escalation Rules

### Automatic Escalation
- **High-Cost Cancellations**: Services >$500/month require executive approval
- **Business-Critical Services**: Critical services need manager review before changes
- **Multi-Department Services**: Services used by multiple departments require coordination
- **Contractual Obligations**: Services with cancellation fees need legal review

### Manual Review Triggers
- **Complex Integrations**: Services with complex system integrations
- **Shared Resources**: Services shared with external partners or clients
- **Regulatory Requirements**: Services required for compliance purposes
- **Custom Configurations**: Heavily customized services that would be difficult to replace
