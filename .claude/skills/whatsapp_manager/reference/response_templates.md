## Escalation Triggers

### Automatic Escalation Keywords
- "CEO", "Manager", "Supervisor"
- "Complaint", "Issue", "Problem"
- "Emergency", "Urgent", "ASAP"
- "Legal", "Lawyer", "Attorney"
- "Contract", "Agreement", "Terms"

### Response Protocols
1. Acknowledge message within 15 minutes
2. Transfer to human operator within 30 minutes
3. Send confirmation to customer about escalation
4. Create ticket in CRM system

## Customer Segmentation Responses

### VIP Customers
- Priority response within 15 minutes
- Personalized greeting with name
- Direct access to premium support

### Regular Customers
- Standard response within 2 hours
- Friendly but professional tone
- Standard support process

### Prospective Customers
- Welcome message with company info
- Prompt for specific interests
- Schedule follow-up if interested
