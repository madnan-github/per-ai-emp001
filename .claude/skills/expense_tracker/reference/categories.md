# Expense Categories and Classifications

## Standard Expense Categories

### Travel Expenses
- **Airfare**: Plane tickets for business travel
- **Lodging**: Hotel stays, Airbnb, etc.
- **Meals**: Business meals during travel
- **Ground Transportation**: Taxis, rideshares, rental cars
- **Parking & Tolls**: Parking fees and tolls
- **Car Rental**: Vehicle rental for business use

### Office Expenses
- **Rent**: Office space rental
- **Utilities**: Electricity, water, internet, phone
- **Office Supplies**: Paper, pens, staplers, etc.
- **Equipment**: Computers, printers, furniture
- **Maintenance**: Cleaning services, repairs

### Meals & Entertainment
- **Business Meals**: Client dinners, business lunches
- **Entertainment**: Tickets to events for business purposes
- **Gifts**: Business gifts to clients/partners

### Marketing & Advertising
- **Online Ads**: Google Ads, Facebook Ads, etc.
- **Print Ads**: Newspaper, magazine advertisements
- **Trade Shows**: Booth fees, travel to shows
- **Promotional Items**: Branded merchandise

### Professional Services
- **Legal**: Attorney fees
- **Accounting**: CPA services, bookkeeping
- **Consulting**: Business consultants
- **Recruitment**: Headhunter fees

### Technology
- **Software**: Licenses, subscriptions
- **Hardware**: Computers, phones, tablets
- **Cloud Services**: AWS, Azure, Google Cloud
- **Telecom**: Phone, internet, data plans

### Insurance
- **General Liability**: Business liability coverage
- **Professional Liability**: Errors and omissions
- **Property Insurance**: Building/equipment coverage
- **Health Insurance**: Employee health benefits

### Payroll & Benefits
- **Salaries**: Employee compensation
- **Benefits**: Health, dental, vision insurance
- **Bonuses**: Performance bonuses
- **Payroll Taxes**: Employer portion of payroll taxes

### Banking & Finance
- **Bank Fees**: Monthly maintenance, transaction fees
- **Loan Interest**: Interest on business loans
- **Credit Card Fees**: Annual fees, processing fees
- **Investment Expenses**: Brokerage fees

### Utilities & Services
- **Electricity**: Power for office/warehouse
- **Water/Sewer**: Water and sewage services
- **Internet**: Internet connectivity
- **Phone**: Landline and mobile services

## Expense Sub-Categories

### Travel Sub-Categories
- **Domestic Travel**: Within the country
- **International Travel**: Outside the country
- **Per Diem**: Daily allowance for meals/lodging
- **Conference Travel**: Travel to conferences
- **Client Visit**: Travel to client locations

### Equipment Sub-Categories
- **Computer Equipment**: PCs, laptops, tablets
- **Office Furniture**: Desks, chairs, filing cabinets
- **Communication**: Phones, headsets, video equipment
- **Maintenance**: Repairs and servicing

### Marketing Sub-Categories
- **Digital Marketing**: Online advertising
- **Traditional Marketing**: Print, radio, TV
- **Social Media**: Platform advertising
- **Content Creation**: Video, photography, copywriting

## Tax-Deductible Categories

### Fully Deductible
- **Ordinary & Necessary**: Business expenses that are ordinary and necessary
- **Travel**: Business travel expenses
- **Office Space**: Rent and utilities for business use
- **Business Meals**: 50% deductible (100% for 2021-2022)

### Partially Deductible
- **Meals**: 50% deductible (varies by jurisdiction)
- **Entertainment**: Generally not deductible
- **Auto Expenses**: Mileage or actual expenses for business use

### Not Deductible
- **Personal Expenses**: Purely personal expenses
- **Political Contributions**: Donations to political campaigns
- **Fines & Penalties**: Legal penalties and fines
- **Life Insurance**: Premiums on policies where business is beneficiary

## Expense Policy Guidelines

### Approval Thresholds
- **Under $50**: Self-approval
- **$50 - $200**: Manager approval
- **$200 - $1,000**: Department head approval
- **$1,000 - $5,000**: VP approval
- **Over $5,000**: Executive approval

### Required Documentation
- **Receipts**: Original receipts required for all expenses
- **Business Purpose**: Clear explanation of business purpose
- **Date & Attendees**: For meals, list attendees and business purpose
- **Pre-approval**: For expenses over $500

### Prohibited Expenses
- **Personal Items**: Clothing, personal electronics
- **Entertainment**: Tickets to sporting events, concerts
- **Gambling**: Casino losses or winnings
- **Club Dues**: Country clubs, social organizations

## Vendor Classifications

### Approved Vendors
- **Preferred Partners**: Pre-negotiated rates and terms
- **Corporate Cards**: Pre-approved vendors for corporate card use
- **Direct Bill**: Vendors that bill directly to the company

### Restricted Vendors
- **Cash Transactions**: Vendors that only accept cash
- **Unverified**: Vendors not yet approved for business use
- **Competitors**: Vendors that compete with the business

## Project Cost Allocation

### Direct Costs
- **Project-Specific**: Costs directly attributable to a project
- **Labor**: Personnel time allocated to specific projects
- **Materials**: Raw materials for specific projects

### Indirect Costs
- **Overhead**: Shared costs allocated across projects
- **Administrative**: General administrative expenses
- **Facilities**: Shared facility costs

## Reporting Categories

### Monthly Reports
- **Operating Expenses**: Day-to-day business expenses
- **Capital Expenditures**: Equipment purchases over $2,500
- **Personnel Costs**: Salaries, benefits, contractor costs

### Quarterly Reports
- **Cost Centers**: Department or division breakdown
- **Project Allocations**: Expenses assigned to specific projects
- **Vendor Spend**: Top vendors by spend category

### Annual Reports
- **Tax Reporting**: Expenses for tax purposes
- **Budget Variance**: Actual vs. budgeted expenses
- **Trend Analysis**: Year-over-year expense trends
