# Email Templates Reference

## Standard Response Templates

### Business Inquiry Response
```
Dear [Name],

Thank you for reaching out regarding [Subject]. We appreciate your interest in our services.

[Custom message based on inquiry]

Best regards,
AI Employee
[Company Name]
```

### Appointment Confirmation Template
```
Dear [Name],

This email confirms your appointment scheduled for [Date] at [Time].

Location: [Address/Virtual Meeting Link]
Duration: [Duration]

Please let us know if you need to reschedule or have any questions.

Best regards,
AI Employee
[Company Name]
```

### Invoice Notification Template
```
Dear [Client Name],

Please find attached your invoice #[Invoice Number] for [Amount] dated [Date].
Payment is due by [Due Date].

[Payment instructions]

Thank you for your business.
[Company Name]
```

## Email Categories and Processing Rules

### High Priority Indicators
- Words: "urgent", "asap", "immediately", "emergency", "critical"
- Sender types: VIP clients, executives, legal entities
- Subject patterns: "[URGENT]", "Important:", "Action Required"

### Automated Response Triggers
- New customer inquiries → Send welcome package
- Support tickets → Acknowledge and assign ticket number
- Payment confirmations → Send receipt and thank you note

## Approval Thresholds
- Invoices > $1000 → Requires management approval
- Contract negotiations → Legal review required
- Sensitive information → Privacy officer approval
